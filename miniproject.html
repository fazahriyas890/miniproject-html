Analysis Result

Confidence: 92%

Plant: Tomato

Diagnosis: Early Blight (Alternaria solani)

Description:

Early blight is a common fungal disease that affects tomato plants. It appears as concentric dark spots on leaves, stems, and fruits.

Recommended Treatment:

  • Remove affected leaves and dispose of them away from garden
  • Apply copper-based fungicide every 7-10 days
  • Water plants at the base to avoid wetting foliage
  • Improve air circulation around plants
  • Apply mulch to prevent soil splash onto leaves

Prevention Tips:

  • Rotate crops annually
  • Choose disease-resistant varieties
  • Space plants properly for air circulation
  • Avoid overhead watering
